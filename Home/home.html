    Our Vision

IEEE SB NIT Raipur is a proud torchbearer of the IEEE community and aspires to actively encourage technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. The SB is constantly pacing towards increasing technological awareness and cultivation a sense of enthusiasm towards innovation. This is undertaken by organizing a variety of events throughout its session which enhance technical expertise while providing international exposure to students.

It creates a technically aware community by dispensing the resources and opportunities that are required to keep students at the top of changes in technology and bring people with a passion for development and also specific interests together. It mentors the next generation of engineers and technologists by carrying the desire learn, interact, collaborate and innovate which make revolutionary engineering advances and hence reshape our world today.
